About Rosy

Certified life coach dedicated to helping people overcome challenges and create meaningful change

My Story

As a certified life coach and motivational speaker, I've dedicated the past 3 years of my life to help people overcome fear and challenges. I've always been fascinated by the idea of inspiring people and bringing positive changes in people's lives.

I finished my schooling in Kathmandu St Mary Boarding School. I was highly passionate and driven from my childhood. My teachers used to say "you will be something great in future, I see that potential in you." Those positive words pushed me to become a better version of myself every day.

I participated in various vocational programs, public speaking, spelling and GK contests. I finished my bachelor's in International Relations back home with honor in 2021.

My Mission

My mission is to empower individuals to take control of their lives. I've experienced significant challenges and transformations in my life. My purpose centers on helping others overcome emotional and mental difficulties by sharing my stories and insights, guiding people to live more fulfilling lives.

I'm deeply passionate about personal development and believe in the power of self-improvement. I want to encourage others to unlock their potential, set meaningful goals, and pursue their dreams.
